Game

For this project, I selected a French-themed palace setting, which I felt was well-suited to the assignment's objectives of guiding the user through various rooms, each featuring different characters. JavaScript functions were implemented to navigate the player through the experience, with a challenge posed in the form of a question. The player had to answer correctly to advance, or risk being returned to the beginning. Throughout the development process, I particularly appreciated the utility of line numbers, which proved invaluable in debugging and organizing the code.
